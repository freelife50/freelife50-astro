<p><strong>Reading time: about 5 minutes</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Hello.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I’m a guy in my 50s from Japan who writes a small blog about everyday life.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Let me ask you something.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Have you ever had this feeling?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“Ah… I have no ideas for this week again.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>You open your computer to write a blog post,<br>but somehow end up doing something completely different.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>That’s exactly what had been happening to me recently.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Well… to be honest,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I had been repeating the same pattern:<br><strong>pretending to start something, and then not actually doing it.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>One evening, though, something suddenly crossed my mind.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>There was something I had been doing almost every single day without fail.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Today, I want to share that small realization.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It’s a little embarrassing,<br>but also kind of funny.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Who this post is for</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>• People who want to keep posting on blogs or social media but feel stuck<br>• People who think, “I don’t really have anything worth sharing”<br>• People in their 50s who want to start something new but hesitate to take the first step<br>• Anyone who wants to make their ordinary daily habits a little more interesting</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:quote -->
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>The realization</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Maybe the things we’re actually good at<br>are hidden in the things we already do every single day.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --></blockquote>
<!-- /wp:quote -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Blogging slowly became a burden</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":3284,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Gemini_Generated_Image_qb8nqqqb8nqqqb8n.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-3284"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Let me be honest.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Writing a blog is harder than it looks.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>In the beginning, everything felt exciting.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“I’ll write about my experiences.”<br>“If even one person finds it helpful, that’s enough.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Back then, the motivation was real.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But after a while, something started to change.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Ideas began to run dry.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>You start thinking things like:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“Didn’t I already write something like this last week?”<br>“Who is even reading this?”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Once those thoughts appear, it gets tough.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Before I knew it, even opening my laptop to write had become a chore.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Updating once a week started to feel difficult.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I would sit in front of my computer…<br>and somehow drift away.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I opened the laptop to write a blog post,<br>but suddenly I was watching YouTube.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And not just anything.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For some reason it was always things like</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>videos of fishmongers cutting fish,<br>or steaming bowls of ramen.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“Wow… that ramen looks amazing.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>While thinking that,<br>I would just sit there watching.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Even though I had opened the computer to write.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Thirty minutes would pass in no time.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And then I realized something.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This is exactly like when I was a student.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>You know that feeling when you’re supposed to study for an exam,<br>but suddenly you start cleaning your room?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Even though you never clean normally.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Yeah… that feeling.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Running away from something unpleasant.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Turns out, I hadn’t changed that much since then.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Taking stock of what I can actually keep doing</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":3279,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Gemini_Generated_Image_bsjb16bsjb16bsjb.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-3279"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>One night, after dinner,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I opened a can of beer and started thinking.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“What are the things I can do every day without effort?”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>So I made a small mental list.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Things that continue easily:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>• Evening drinks (beer time)<br>• Swimming<br>• Waking up early<br>• Walking my Shiba Inu, Momiji</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Things that somehow never continue:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>• Blogging<br>• Studying<br>• Reading</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Looking at that list, one thing became obvious.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Apparently, I’m bad at continuing things that require a lot of thinking.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But then,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>as I took a sip of beer,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>something clicked.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“Ah… this is it.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Somehow, evening drinks always continue</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":3280,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Gemini_Generated_Image_t6xlfct6xlfct6xl.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-3280"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>My evening drink.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This one thing happens almost every day.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Rain or shine.<br>Even when I’m a little tired.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I stand in the kitchen,<br>make a simple snack with whatever is in the fridge,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>and open my first beer.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>That very first sip.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A little bitter.<br>A little cold.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And suddenly I realized something.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I might actually be a <strong>true kitchen drinker.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">“Alright… I’ll become a beer livestreamer.”</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":3269,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Gemini_Generated_Image_k45dxak45dxak45d.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-3269"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A while ago I heard someone say something interesting.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“Do what you’re good at.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>That phrase suddenly popped into my head.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Honestly, I have no idea if anyone would want to watch it.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I don’t know if there’s any demand<br>for a middle-aged guy quietly drinking beer in his kitchen.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But there is one thing I know for sure.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I can do it <strong>every single day.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>That thought suddenly fit perfectly in my mind.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Like a puzzle piece snapping into place.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“Alright… I’ll become a beer livestreamer.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Kind of like those tourism commercials that say<br>“Let’s go to Kyoto.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The idea is simple.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>No need to show my face.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Just standing in the kitchen,<br>making a small snack,<br>and drinking beer quietly.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>No script.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>No special skills.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Just a can of beer<br>and whatever ingredients happen to be in the fridge.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But for some reason,<br>this feels like something I can keep doing.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Maybe I’ll film my walks with Momiji too</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":3286,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Gemini_Generated_Image_4d1xlq4d1xlq4d1x.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-3286"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Once I started thinking about it,<br>my motivation suddenly came back.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>So I even ordered a neck holder for my smartphone.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The idea is to hang the phone around my neck<br>and record videos while walking my Shiba Inu, Momiji.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>In the evening air,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Momiji walks along the street with her little steps.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Her fur looks firm,<br>but when you touch it, it’s surprisingly soft.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And just like that,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>a middle-aged YouTuber is born.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Well… maybe.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Although there is one problem.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Whenever I point a camera at her,<br>she immediately looks away.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But honestly,<br>that might be part of the content too.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Conclusion</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":3282,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Gemini_Generated_Image_cu6lwxcu6lwxcu6l.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-3282"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>On the night I ran out of blog ideas,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>while opening a can of beer,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I realized something.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The things we can do every day<br>are often so ordinary that we don’t even notice them.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It’s not about being talented.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It’s about being able to continue.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Not about being praised by others,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>but about enjoying it yourself.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Maybe the thing you do every day</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>without earning money<br>and without anyone praising you</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>is actually your version of <strong>“evening drinks.”</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>There’s a phrase people often say:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“Today is the youngest you’ll ever be again.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Now that I’m in my 50s,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>starting something new often comes with comments like,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“Isn’t it a bit late for that?”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But if you ignore that “too late” voice for a moment,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>you might discover something interesting waiting there.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Anyway,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>tonight I’ll probably be back in the kitchen again.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Beer in hand.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Just another <strong>kitchen drinker.</strong> 🍺</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
