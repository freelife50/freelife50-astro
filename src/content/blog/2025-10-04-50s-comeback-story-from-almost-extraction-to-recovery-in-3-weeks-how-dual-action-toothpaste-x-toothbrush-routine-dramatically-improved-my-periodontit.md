<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>4. My “Dual Routine” — Morning, Daytime, Night Practice</strong></h2>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/Gemini_Generated_Image_4hizgw4hizgw4hiz.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-2413"/></figure>

<p><strong>Common Rule:</strong><strong><br></strong> Always use <strong>floss or interdental brush before brushing</strong><strong><br></strong> 👉 Removing debris first is the key to letting active ingredients work.</p>

<p><strong>“The basic routine: interdental brush → small brush → large soft brush.”</strong></p>

<p><strong>&nbsp;“Toothpaste? Just a dab. The real key is how often you brush — morning, noon, night, or whenever you feel like it.”</strong></p>

<p><strong>【for example】</strong></p>

<p><strong>Morning: Reset &amp; Prepare (3–4 minutes)</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list"><li>Floss / interdental brush<br></li>

<li>Seiyouba EX × small brush (one-tuft)<br>→ Pinpoint between teeth, gum line<br></li>

<li>SP-T Gel × large soft brush (ultra-fine bristles)<br>→ Gentle sweeping + gum massage<br></li>

<li>Rinse only <strong>once</strong><strong><br></strong></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Daytime: Refresh (flexible style)</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list"><li>Floss / interdental brush<br></li>

<li>Basic: Seiyouba EX<br><ul class="wp-block-list"><li>Sometimes with one-tuft for quick touch-up<br></li>

<li>Other times with large soft brush for light refresh<br>👉 Choose depending on mood — makes it sustainable.<br></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Night: Full Defense &amp; Repair (4–5 minutes)</strong></p>

<ul class="wp-block-list"><li>Floss / interdental brush<br></li>

<li>SP-T Gel × large soft brush<br>→ Focus on periodontal pockets with gentle strokes<br></li>

<li>One-tuft brush for weak spots<br></li>

<li>Include gum massage<br></li>

<li>Rinse only once (leave a light taste)<br></li>
</ul>

<li>Daytime = optional bonus refresh</li>
