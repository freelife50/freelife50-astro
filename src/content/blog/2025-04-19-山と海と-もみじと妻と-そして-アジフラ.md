<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>A quiet day with my wife and our Shiba Inu Momiji — surrounded by mountains, cherry blossoms, and the sea.</strong><br>Sometimes, the best rewards are simple: crispy fried fish and a cold beer after a good walk.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --></blockquote>

<p><strong>冒頭文/ introduction</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:quote -->
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>日常にちょっと疲れたとき、海と山、そして静かな春の光の中で過ごす時間は、心をふっと軽くしてくれます。<br>そんなある日、妻ともみじと一緒に、ちいさな“ごほうび時間”を見つけに行ってきました</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>When life feels a bit heavy, a quiet spring day with mountains, the sea, and loved ones can lighten your heart.<br>That’s exactly what we went looking for—just my wife, Momiji, and me.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --></blockquote>
<!-- /wp:quote -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">本文/body</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>最近、海も山も楽しめるような場所に行きたくて、 妻と柴犬もみじを連れて「二宮 吾妻山公園」へ行ってきました。<br><em>Lately, I’ve been craving somewhere that offers both the sea and the mountains. So we visited Azumayama Park in Ninomiya, just the three of us: my wife, our Shiba Inu Momiji, and me.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":85,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/image-14-1024x798.png" alt="" class="wp-image-85"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>Caption: 春の芝生広場、家族連れでにぎやか / Families enjoying spring sunshine on the lawn.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>もみじは水が大好き。<br><em>Momiji loves water.</em><br>山登りはあまり好きじゃないのか、いつも途中で「もう帰ろうよ」という目で見てくるんですが、<br><em>But when it comes to hiking uphill, she tends to look at me like, “Can we go home yet?”</em><br>海や川があるとスイッチが入って、全力ダッシュ＆全力ハッスルになります（笑）<br><em>Once she sees the sea or a stream, though, she flips a switch — full sprint, full joy!</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>この日は春の陽気で、桜も見頃。<br><em>The cherry blossoms were in full bloom under the warm spring sun.</em><br>芝生には家族連れがたくさんいて、あちこちから聞こえる子どもたちの笑い声に、ふと平和を感じました。<br><em>Children’s laughter echoed across the park — a peaceful moment that felt almost luxurious.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":86,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/image-15-1024x769.png" alt="" class="wp-image-86"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>Caption: 海も桜も楽しめる、絶好の季節 / The perfect season to enjoy both cherry blossoms and ocean views.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>実は最近、体力的に妻に追いつけなくなってきていて、山歩きでもちょっとしんどくなることがあります。<br><em>These days, I struggle to keep up with my wife during hikes.</em><br>妻は僕以上に歩くのが好きで、どんどん先に行ってしまう。<br><em>She walks ahead, seemingly tireless.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>だから、今回は僕でも無理なく登れそうな場所を選びました。<br><em>So this time, I picked a trail I could manage without overdoing it.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>目標は、もう一度一緒に山歩きを楽しめるくらいに体力をつけること。<br><em>My goal is to rebuild enough stamina to enjoy walking alongside her again.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>そのために今、10kmマラソンへの再挑戦と、ダイエットにも挑んでいます。<br><em>I'm also training for a 10km run — and working on my weight.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>今の体重は……たぶん74kgくらい（怖くて最近測ってません）。<br><em>I probably weigh around 74kg (haven’t dared to step on the scale lately).</em> </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>目指すは65kg。でも68kgまで落とせたらいいなぁと思ってます。<br><em>My target is 65kg, but I’d settle for 68kg.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":87,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/image-16-769x1024.png" alt="" class="wp-image-87"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>Caption: 海辺でテンションMAXなもみじ / Momiji running full speed by the seaside.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>帰りは、高速を使って我が家のプリウス（走行距離19万キロ超）でサクッと帰宅。<br><em>We headed home via the expressway in our Prius, which has now clocked over 190,000 km.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>道中はあいかわらず、夫婦の会話は少なめ。<strong>でも、それが僕たちにはちょうどよかったりするんです。</strong><em>As usual, we didn’t talk much on the way back — <strong>but that quiet suits us just fine.</strong></em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>お昼ごはんは、公園ではとらずに、帰り道の「角上魚類」でアジフライをテイクアウト。<br><em>No lunch at the park. Instead, we stopped by Kakujo Gyoryu and picked up some aji fry.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>家に帰って、揚げたてサクサクのアジフライをかじりながら、もちろんビールも一緒にいただきました。<br><em>Back home, it was crispy fried horse mackerel and a cold beer — a perfect combo.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>運動したあとって、なんであんなにビールが美味しいんだろう。<br><em>Why is beer after exercise always so good?</em> </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>「今日はたくさん歩いたし、まあいいか」</strong>って、自分に甘くなるのも50代あるあるです。<br><em><strong>“I walked a lot today, so it’s fine” </strong>— classic 50s thinking.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":88,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/image-17-1024x769.png" alt="" class="wp-image-88"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>Caption: またこの景色に、会いに行こう / Let’s come back to this view someday.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>次はどこに行こうかな。<br><em>Now I’m wondering where to go next.</em><br>それまでにちょっとでも体力をつけて、また妻やもみじと一緒にのんびり歩けるように。<br><em>Hopefully I’ll be a little stronger next time — enough to enjoy another walk with my wife and Momiji.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>…そして、もみじがじっと海を見つめていた。そんな何気ない時間が、実は一番の贅沢なのかもしれない。…And there she was—Momiji, quietly gazing out at the sea.<br>Perhaps these ordinary moments are the truest kind of luxury.</p>
