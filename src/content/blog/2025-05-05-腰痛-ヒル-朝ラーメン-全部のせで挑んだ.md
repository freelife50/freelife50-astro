<p>🍜Back Pain, Leeches, and Morning Ramen – A Fully Loaded Children's Day Hike</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>✅この記事で伝えたいこと/purpose</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:quote -->
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>この記事では、こどもの日に「腰痛・朝ラーメン・ヒルの恐怖」という“全部のせ”状態で登山に挑んだ一日を、ユーモアと共に綴っています。身体の声に耳を傾けながらも、あえて動いてみることで見えてきた気づき。<br>「なんか調子悪いな…でもちょっと外に出てみようかな」と思ってる人に届けばうれしいです。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>On Children’s Day, I went hiking—with a heavy belly, back pain, and fear of leeches.<br>This post reflects how even on the most chaotic days, stepping outside can shift your body, your mind, and maybe your life.<br>Sometimes, a fully loaded day brings unexpected clarity.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --></blockquote>
<!-- /wp:quote -->

<p><strong>🟨【冒頭文 / Introduction】</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:quote -->
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>「こどもの日やし、ちょっと山でも行ってみるか〜」と軽い気持ちで出発したら、<br>腰痛・朝ラーメン・ヒルの恐怖まで全部のせでついてきた一日。<br>でも、なんやかんやで「行ってよかった」と思えた話です。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>On Children’s Day, I casually said, “Let’s go for a hike.”<br>What I got instead was a fully loaded combo of back pain, a belly full of ramen, and leech anxiety.<br>Still… somehow, I ended up feeling glad I went. This is that story.</p>

<p>☀️<strong>導入文/opening</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:quote -->
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>2025年5月5日。世間は「こどもの日」やけど、僕の心は「腰痛の日」やった。<br>朝起きた瞬間から腰がうずく。<br>そういえばこの前、整骨院で月額56,000円の集中ケアコースを勧められたばかり。<br>来週までに返事をせなあかん。でも…高ない？ もったいない？<br>「整形外科で保険効かせた方がええかなぁ…」と悩みながら、気づけば車に乗ってた。<br>向かう先は山。しかも、腰痛のくせに。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>May 5th—Children’s Day in Japan.<br>But for me, it felt more like "Back Pain Day."<br>From the moment I woke up, my lower back was throbbing.<br>Just a few days ago, a chiropractor recommended a monthly ¥56,000 treatment course.<br>The deadline to decide is next week, but it feels expensive... kind of a waste.<br>I even thought, “Maybe I should just go to an orthopedic clinic and use insurance.”<br>And yet, somehow, I found myself getting in the car—headed to the mountains. With back pain.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --></blockquote>
<!-- /wp:quote -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">🍜まずは朝から“背徳のフルコース”</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":376,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/9a554d41d808f8f2014d73e20c0fe656.png" alt="" class="wp-image-376"/></figure>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>（ラーメンの写真撮るの忘れました。すみません。I forgot to take a photo. Sorry about that.）</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>登山の前に、なぜか朝ラーメン。しかも替え玉2回にライスまで追加。<br>もはや“罪のフルコース”。腹も心も満たされて、でもその分、体はズッシリ。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>Before the hike, for some reason, I had morning ramen.<br>Not just one bowl—two extra servings of noodles and rice on the side.<br>It was the ultimate guilty full course.<br>My heart felt satisfied, but my body was weighed down.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">🧟‍♂️ヒル、出るんちゃうん？</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":377,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none","align":"center"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/PXL_20250505_005347233-scaled.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-377"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>この時期、ヒルが出るって話はよう聞く。特に丹沢では何度か痛い目を見てる。<br>ちょっとトラウマ気味やから、念のためAIでヒル出現率を予測してみた。<br>結果は「出現確率は低め」。でも、「ゼロじゃない」ってのが逆に怖いねん。<br>「もしかして…」って思うと、足元が気になってしゃあない。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>Around this time of year, leeches start appearing.<br>I’ve had my fair share of nasty encounters in Tanzawa, so I’m a bit traumatized.<br>Just to be safe, I had AI predict the leech appearance rate for today.<br>The answer? “Low probability.”<br>But not zero. And that “not zero” messes with your head.<br>Once you start thinking about it, every step feels risky.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">🚗車内で葛藤。整骨院か？整形外科か？いや、山か？</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":378,"width":"535px","height":"auto","sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none","align":"center"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-large is-resized"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/PXL_20250505_005010361-scaled.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-378" style="width:535px;height:auto"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>車内ではまだ悩んでいた。治療に金をかけるか、自然に頼るか。<br>そんなことを考えてる間に山のふもとに到着。<br>途中「崩落のため通行止め」の看板を見て、ちょっとホッとする。<br>「今日はやめとこかな…」と思った瞬間、妻が言う。「行こか」<br>…はい、決定。もみじも準備万端やし。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>In the car, I was still stuck—should I pay for treatment, or trust nature to heal me?<br>While I was overthinking, we arrived at the trailhead.<br>There was a sign: “Trail closed due to landslide.” I felt a strange relief.<br>Just as I thought, “Maybe we should skip it,” my wife casually said, “Let’s go.”<br>And that settled it. Our dog Momiji was already eager and ready.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>腰痛・満腹・ヒルの恐怖。まさに“全部のせトッピング”状態。<br>崩れた橋、ぬかるんだ道も、妻ともみじはズンズン進んでいく。<br>暑さに弱いもみじも、今日は涼しくて快調。<br>川のせせらぎ、鳥のさえずり。山の静けさが心を癒してくれる。<br>僕はと言えば、ラーメンがこみ上げそうになりながら後ろをトボトボ。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>Back pain, ramen belly, fear of leeches. It was the ultimate “everything-loaded” hike.<br>Even a broken bridge and muddy trails couldn’t stop my wife and Momiji.<br>Momiji, who usually struggles with heat, was in great shape thanks to the cool weather.<br>The sound of the stream, the birds chirping—the calmness of the mountains healed my spirit.<br>Meanwhile, I was dragging behind, ramen trying to make a comeback.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">🗻風が吹く山頂で、ふと気づいた</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":381,"width":"444px","height":"auto","sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none","align":"center"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-large is-resized"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/PXL_20250505_014954307-scaled.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-381" style="width:444px;height:auto"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>頂上にたどり着いたとき、風がサーッと通り抜けた。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":382,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none","align":"center"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/image-4.png" alt="" class="wp-image-382"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>「あれ？ 腰、ちょっと楽になってる…？」<br>ショック療法か？気のせいか？それとも「動くこと」が効いたのか。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>At the summit, a cool breeze swept over us.<br>I suddenly noticed—“Wait, my back… it feels a bit better?”<br>Was it shock therapy? Just mental? Or maybe, moving really helped?</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">🚶‍♂️そして帰り道、気づくこと</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>駐車場に戻ってくる頃、なんだか腰が軽い。<br>あんなに痛かったのに、少しマシになってる。<br>座ってばかりの生活が、逆にあかんかったんかも。<br>ちょっとでも動くことの大切さ、身にしみて感じた一日やった。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>By the time we got back to the car, my back felt lighter.<br>The pain that had bothered me all morning had eased up.<br>Maybe sitting around all the time was actually worse.<br>Today reminded me how even a little movement can make a big difference.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">🍺今日も“全部のせ”でよかったやん</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>今日の登山は、まさに「全部のせ」。<br>腰痛、ヒル、満腹、暑さ、崩落…でも、結果オーライ。<br>「ちょっと外に出るだけで、人生って少し動き出す」<br>たまには全部のせでも、ええもんやで。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>Today’s hike had it all—back pain, leeches, ramen overload, heat, landslides.<br>But in the end, it turned out just fine.<br>Sometimes, all it takes is stepping outside to get your life moving again.<br>And sometimes, a fully loaded day is exactly what you need.</em></p>
