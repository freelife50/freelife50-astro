<p><strong>🌍 English Reflection:</strong><br>I never imagined I'd one day raise children.<br>Even as a father, I wasn’t always confident or reliable.<br>But those tiny hands reaching out after a bath,<br>saying “welcome home”—they reminded me I was never truly alone.</p>
