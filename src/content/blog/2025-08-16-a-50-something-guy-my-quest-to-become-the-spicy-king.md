<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Chapter 1: The Wall of Ordering, and the Kitchen Guy</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":2100,"sizeSlug":"full","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Google_AI_Studio_2025-08-16T12_52_49.533Z.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-2100"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For years, I’ve dreamed of this moment. On my “things to do before I die” list, this one is at the very top:<br><strong>Hokkyoku Ramen at 10x spiciness.</strong><strong><br></strong> It’s the Everest of Nakamoto. No oxygen tank, just pure suffering.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But let’s be honest—the hardest part isn’t the ramen. It’s ordering it.<br>I’ve tried up to 7x before, but 10x? That’s uncharted territory.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And then comes the dreaded moment:<br>“<strong>Hokkyoku Ramen, 10x spice, coming up!!</strong>”<br>The “coming up” part gets yelled with extra volume, and the whole shop falls silent.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>That’s when I wished for the future of silent tablet ordering.<br>“This is my private battle, don’t announce it to the world!”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Still, I clenched my free “extra-large serving” coupon.<br>But inside my head, a voice whispered:<br>“Are you sure you can even finish it?”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And then—snap. Something flipped inside.<br>I entered “the zone.”<br>“<strong>Hokkyoku Ramen, 10x spice, EXTRA-LARGE, with veggies!!</strong>”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Nearby diners turned to look.<br>Their eyes said it all: <em>“This guy… is he trying to die?”</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Even the kitchen chef, a man about my age, stopped mid-motion and glanced at me.<br>His eyes clearly asked:<br>“Can this old dude really handle it?”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For a moment, the whole shop froze.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Supplement 1: What Is Hokkyoku Ramen?</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>(Official site:<a href="https://nakamoto.tokyo/"> https://nakamoto.tokyo/</a>)<br>The spiciest and most infamous dish at Mōko Tanmen Nakamoto.<br>“Hokkyoku” doesn’t mean cold—it means <em>“the polar extreme of spice.”</em><em><br></em> The soup is miso-based, packed with chili and spicy paste until it turns into magma.<br>For ordinary people, finishing it is impossible. Even for spice maniacs, it’s more like “training” than eating.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Supplement 3: Hokkyoku 10x vs. Veggie Hokkyoku 10x</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Hokkyoku 10x</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Thick, almost paste-like soup<br></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Noodles fused with chili, impossible to slurp<br></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>One bite = direct damage to lips, throat, stomach<br></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Sweetness “slightly” softens the pain (but honestly, meaningless)<br></li>

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Tongue enters “taste bud shutdown mode” instantly<br></li>

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Chapter 2: The First Battle — Veggie Hokkyoku 10x (Extra-Large)</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For my first attempt, I chose the veggie version. Why?<br>Because I’m 50, so I thought: <em>“At least add some veggies for health.”</em><em><br></em> (Yeah, eating lava noodles and still thinking “health.” Ridiculous, right?)</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":2095,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/PXL_20250628_050135471-1024x769.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-2095"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong><br></strong> → Soup: magma. Veggies: crunchy but useless.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The first bite—fire in my lips and throat.<br>I couldn’t slurp, so I chewed the noodles like spaghetti.<br>My mouth no longer knew what was veggie and what was noodle.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But then… adrenaline kicked in.<br>I was high.<br>Not “spicy but tasty”—more like <em>“pain but tasty.”</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I kept going, and somehow, I finished!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":2096,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/PXL_20250628_051251439-1024x769.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-2096"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong><br></strong> → Bowl looked like a battlefield, covered in chili remnants.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>On the way home on my bike, the summer wind felt strangely refreshing.<br>That was the wind of victory.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Chapter 3: The True Path — Pure Hokkyoku 10x (Extra-Large)</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A few days later, something nagged at me.<br>“…Can I really call myself the Spicy King?”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>After all, I only beat the veggie version. The sweetness was like training wheels.<br>“No. To be king, I must face the real thing.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Back at Nakamoto, I muttered as I walked in:<br><strong>“I’ll be back.”</strong> (Was that Terminator? RoboCop? Who cares.)</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“<strong>Hokkyoku Ramen, 10x spice, EXTRA-LARGE!!</strong>”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":2097,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/PXL_20250701_033127329-1024x769.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-2097"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong><br></strong> → Pure red surface, oil floating like a declaration of war.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>First bite—my brain screamed danger, alarms blaring.<br>Flavor turned into pain. My insides whimpered.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But my pride, my stubborn 50-year-old gut said:<br>“Don’t underestimate decades of reckless eating!”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Sweat poured, lips burned, yet I kept going… until finally—finished!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":2098,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/PXL_20250701_034031646-1024x769.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-2098"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong><br></strong> → Red chili coating the bowl like victory flags.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Inside, a voice shouted:<br><strong>“You are now the true Spicy King!”</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Chapter 4: Victory and Its Price</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":2101,"sizeSlug":"full","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Google_AI_Studio_2025-08-16T13_00_02.195Z.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-2101"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Two bowls of 10x (extra-large), back-to-back victories.<br>Kitchen guy, did you see that?<br>This old man still has it!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>He simply said: “Thank you.”<br>But I swear, hidden in his tone was: <em>“Respect.”</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Outside, the air felt amazing.<br>But that night, round two began.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Intense stomach pain.<br>Rushing to the toilet, muttering:<br>“<strong><span class="bold-red">…Hot…</span></strong>”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Sweat dripping, heart pounding.<br>Yes, this was the price of unlocking my limiter.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Yet—I had no regrets.<br>As Raoh once said:<br><strong>“I have no regrets in my life.”</strong></p>
