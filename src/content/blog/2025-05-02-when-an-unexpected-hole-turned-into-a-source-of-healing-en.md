<h3 class="wp-block-heading">A Small Incident One Morning</h3>

<p>It started like any other workday.<br>A colleague caught their foot on a chair leg and stumbled — and somehow, the impact sent their foot straight into the wall.<br>Before anyone fully processed what had happened, <strong>there was a hole in the wall.</strong> 🕳️</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Blame? Laugh? Or… Something Else?</h3>

<p>Nobody was hurt — that was the main thing — and the atmosphere in the room shifted quickly.<br>The tension dissolved into something lighter. Laughter, almost.</p>

<p>Instead of blaming or scolding, we laughed. And then someone said:</p>

<p><strong>"Should we just stick something over it?"</strong></p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">The "Healing Wallpaper Project" — with Chappy's Help</h3>

<p>So we brought in Chappy (ChatGPT) and decided to make a custom A4 patch to cover the hole with something cheerful.</p>

<p>"How about a Shiba Inu peeking through the hole?"</p>

<p>That was Chappy's suggestion — and it was perfect. A Shiba Inu character that looked a little like our beloved Momiji, nose-first through the wall, peering out at whoever walked past.</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-large is-resized"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/shiba_peeking_compressed.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-316" style="width:278px;height:auto"/></figure>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">We Put It Up — and It Was a Hit</h3>

<p>We printed it out and stuck it on the wall. And this is what it looks like now 👇</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-large is-resized"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/PXL_20250502_082451874-scaled.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-317" style="width:399px;height:auto"/></figure>

<p>People walking past would stop, tilt their heads, and grin.<br>Some smiled. Some giggled. Some took photos.<br><strong>The healing effect was real.</strong></p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">A Small Reminder About Blogging — and Life</h3>

<p>These little everyday moments — the accidents, the workarounds, the tiny sparks of creativity — are actually the heart of what I write about here.</p>

<p>Mistakes don't always need to be fixed.<br><strong>Sometimes, they just need a bit of warmth.</strong></p>

<p>That was the mood in the office that day. And honestly, it's the mood I try to bring to every post.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">A Quiet Presence on the Wall</h3>

<p>Even now, that little Shiba Inu — or someone very much like Momiji — is still up there, peeking through the wall at anyone who passes.</p>

<p>Let the hole be a reminder:<br><strong>Healing always comes from unexpected places.</strong> 🐶</p>
