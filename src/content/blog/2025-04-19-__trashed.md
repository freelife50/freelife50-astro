<!-- wp:quote -->
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p><strong>この記事では、運動・仕事・食事・芸術と、50代の一日に訪れた“気づき”を綴っています。</strong><br>整備された淵野辺公園でのランニング、夜のステーキ、そして翌朝ふと出会った岡本太郎の作品——<br>何気ない日常に潜むインスピレーションと、歩くことで得られる新しい視点を、写真とともにご紹介します。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p><strong>A full day of motion, meals, and meaning — this story captures how a quiet morning walk led to an unexpected encounter with art and reflection.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --></blockquote>
<!-- /wp:quote -->

<p><span class="bold-red"><strong>🕒 このブログは約2〜3分で読めます。<br>🕒 This article takes about 2 to 3 minutes to read</strong></span>.</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>冒頭文/introduction</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:quote -->
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>運動して、働いて、ご飯を食べて、また歩く。<br>そんな何気ない日々の中に、ふと心に残る「気づき」がある——<br>そんなことを実感した、ある50代の一日。</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Run, work, eat, walk.<br>In these ordinary routines, sometimes a quiet realization stays with you.<br>This was one of those days.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph --></blockquote>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">本文/body</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>昨日はなんだか濃い一日だった。<br><strong>Yesterday turned out to be quite a full day.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>午前中は、舗装されて走りやすくなった<strong>淵野辺公園</strong>でジョギングとウォーキングを繰り返しながら、気持ちよく汗を流した。<br><strong>In the morning, I enjoyed a mix of jogging and walking at Fuchinobe Park, which recently got its paths beautifully repaved.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>以前は砂利道だったけど、最近きれいに整備されて、足にもやさしく、ランナーにはありがたい環境に。<br><strong>It used to be a gravel path, but now it's much easier on the feet—perfect for runners like me.</strong></p>

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>午後は仕事。<br><strong>After that, it was off to work for the afternoon.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>忙しかったけれど、夜には職場の友人と<strong>久々のステーキディナー</strong>へ。<br><strong>After work, I met up with a colleague for a long-awaited steak dinner.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>食べて、話して、笑って、あっという間のひととき。<br><strong>We ate, talked, laughed—it was one of those dinners that fly by.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>写真を撮るのを忘れるくらい夢中だった。<br><strong>So much so, I completely forgot to take a photo!</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>そして、翌朝。<br><strong>Then came the next morning.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>昨日のステーキの罪悪感（？）を少しでも和らげるために、<strong>自宅から相模原駅までウォーキング</strong>。<br><strong>Feeling slightly guilty about all that steak, I decided to walk from home to Sagamihara Station.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>ゆっくりと歩くと、普段気にも留めなかった景色が目に入ってくる。<br><strong>Taking it slow helped me notice things I usually overlook.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>📷 <em>[Morning walk log – 3.19km, 40 mins]</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>その途中、視界に飛び込んできたのが、あの<strong>赤い手と青い手の彫刻</strong>。<br><strong>That’s when I saw something striking—red and blue hands reaching out from the ground.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>「…あれ、もしかして岡本太郎？」<br><strong>Wait… could this be a Taro Okamoto piece?</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>そう思って近づいてみたら、やっぱり。<br><strong>I went closer, and sure enough—it was.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>作品名は**「呼ぶ 赤い手・青い手」**。<br><strong>The piece is called “The Calling Red Hand / Blue Hand.”</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"className":""} -->
<p>まるで昨日の“食・運動・反省”を見透かされたようで、不思議と胸に残った。<br><strong>It felt like those hands were calling out to me, reflecting everything I did the day before—my indulgence, my exercise, and even my morning guilt.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
