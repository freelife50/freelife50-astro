<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Why Did We Even Go to Mt. Takao Together?</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":3209,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Gemini_Generated_Image_kfccydkfccydkfcc.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-3209"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Let me start a little earlier.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>My workplace recently celebrated a milestone anniversary.<br>For some reason, I was selected as a member of the organizing committee.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>At one meeting, the topic of creating a company mascot came up.<br>Someone mentioned that my son was studying at an art university.<br>Before I knew it, he had agreed to design the character.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>That led to him being invited to the anniversary ceremony.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>At the after-party, purely by chance,<br>he ended up sitting at the same table as several guests who often hike Mt. Takao.<br>And he casually promised them,<br>“Let’s climb Mt. Takao together sometime.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The guests were men my age or older.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>If I had been his age,<br>I probably wouldn’t have made that promise.<br>(I’ve always been shy.)</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Fearless? Unreserved?<br>Is this what they call Generation Z?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I was impressed.<br>And, honestly, a little conflicted.<br>I felt like I’d lost to my own kid.</p>

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Summary — Coincidence, or a Chain of Actions?</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":3245,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Gemini_Generated_Image_1kp06h1kp06h1kp0.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-3245"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Looking back, the day was full of “coincidences.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I was coincidentally chosen for the committee.<br>The mascot idea came up.<br>My son happened to study design.<br>He happened to accept.<br>He happened to attend the ceremony.<br>He happened to sit with hikers.<br>He happened to promise to climb Mt. Takao.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Remove any one step,<br>and the reunion would not have happened.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Is that fate?</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Maybe.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Or maybe we just give meaning afterward.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But one thing is certain:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>If we hadn’t moved,<br>none of it would have happened.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Accepting the design job.<br>Showing up.<br>Meeting people face to face.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Those small actions<br>become something that later feels like guidance.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The hike was 3.1 km up, 4.1 km down.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>On paper, that’s all.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But what remains with me<br>is standing beside my son watching Mt. Fuji,<br>being pulled by Momiji,<br>my knees aching on the stone pavement,<br>and that unexpected reunion.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>If there’s an invitation you’re hesitating about,<br>a promise you’re about to ignore—</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>maybe there’s a future connection waiting there.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>You don’t have to do something big.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Move a little.<br>Meet someone.<br>Show up.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Mt. Takao may be a famous tourist mountain.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But for me,<br>it became a place that reminded me:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Connections are born where we go to meet them.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And maybe—</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>the future isn’t random at all.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>It’s built</strong><strong><br></strong><strong> from small choices,</strong><strong><br></strong><strong> one step at a time.</strong></p>
