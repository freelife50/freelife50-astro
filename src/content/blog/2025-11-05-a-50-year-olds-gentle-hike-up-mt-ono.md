<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>🚗 Starting from Yamakita Tsuburano Park</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":2651,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none","align":"center"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/PXL_20251102_052024629.MP_.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-2651"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A <strong>free parking lot</strong> makes it easy for drivers.<br>From there, the trail gently climbs about <strong>2.6 km</strong>,<br>taking <strong>70–80 minutes</strong> to reach the summit.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Even with light shoes and casual wear, you’ll be fine.<br>And as you walk, pampas grass waves in the autumn wind,<br>the golden field shining like a movie scene.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>💧 Rest Spot Before the Climb</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Next to the parking lot, there’s a <strong>rest area with water, benches, and toilets</strong>.<br>Clean, quiet, and perfect for stretching or preparing before you start.<br>It gives a small-town comfort that says, “Take your time.”</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>⚠️ Tips &amp; Precautions</strong></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Check <strong>parking gate hours</strong> in advance.<br></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Watch for <strong>narrow forest roads</strong> when driving.<br></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Some <strong>slippery sections</strong> – wear good shoes.<br></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Take it slow and watch the weather.<br></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>From spring to autumn</strong>, bring <strong>bug spray and long sleeves</strong> for leeches.<br></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>If you go prepared, the mountain rewards you with calm joy.</p>
