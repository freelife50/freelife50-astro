<p>■<strong>Buying <em>One Way</em> as a Luxury</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Car + driver: <strong>$35 (one way)</strong> / <strong>$63 (round-trip)</strong><strong><br></strong> I skipped the round-trip discount to keep my return time <em>free</em>.<br>I paid $7 more for the luxury of not being tied down.<br>That’s what I call <strong>“buying freedom.”</strong><strong><br></strong></li>
</ul>



<p>■<strong>The Ride</strong></p>



<p>Spacious lounge, calm seas, and&#8230; gray skies.<br>The rain blurred the horizon, but there was still a certain peace in that.<br>Even on a holiday, the ferry wasn’t crowded—most people just take the Aqua-Line bridge now.</p>

<p>Even in the rain, the sound of the engine and waves feels soothing.<br>Sometimes, the quiet moments when “nothing happens” are the most luxurious part of a journey.</p>

<p>■<strong>Getting There</strong></p>



<p>The road is <strong>extremely narrow</strong>—two cars can’t pass.<br>Drive carefully; once there, the parking lot is surprisingly big and empty.</p>



<p>■<strong>The Walk</strong></p>



<p>Round-trip about 2.2 km.<br>After rain, it’s muddy and slippery. Momiji tried to chase a crab—classic dog move.<br>My wife said, “Since we’re here, let’s walk.”<br>It wasn’t “just a short walk.” It was a <em>mini-hike.</em></p>



<p>Still, the silence and old legend of the <em>Eight Dog Warriors (Hakkenden)</em> gave the place a mystical charm.</p>



<p><strong>Tips – Fushihime Cave</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Narrow road: avoid large vehicles.<br></li>



<li>Watch your step; go only if you really want to.<br></li>



<li>Go during daylight; it’s eerily quiet.<br></li>
</ul>

<p>■<strong>📍Practical Summary (Save This!)</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Ferry:</strong> $35 one way (car+driver) / $63 round-trip. Round-trip is cheaper, but limits flexibility.<br></li>



<li><strong>Hotta Elementary School:</strong> Stay $28 / Bath $3.5. No dinner service—bring your own or dine out.<br></li>



<li><strong>Fushihime Cave:</strong> Narrow road + muddy trail. Skip if unsure.<br></li>



<li><strong>Nomizo Falls:</strong> Waterproof shoes essential. “Heart reflection” visible only under rare conditions.<br></li>



<li><strong>Dog-Friendly:</strong> Use terraces, carry towels and water. Beware slippery spots near water.<br></li>
</ul>
