<p>0°C. Light snow.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>I hesitated.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But walking warmed my body.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The clear stream of Maedagawa.<br>The white heron.<br>Cherry blossoms and daffodils.<br>The wild boar warning sign.<br>The animals at Arinkurin.<br>The quiet weight of Daizenji.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>And the Lunch Pack in the wind and snow.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Life might be similar.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Perfect timing rarely comes.<br>Weather, mood, physical condition — rarely aligned.</p>

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>But perhaps only the days we move, even while hesitating,<br>remain in memory.</p>

<p>And the warmest thing that day?</p>
