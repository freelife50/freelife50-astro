<p><strong>目的文/purpose statement</strong></p>

<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><p>この記事では、マラソン前日にも関わらずクラフトビールフェスに参加した50代の僕が、「無理せず、でも楽しむ」生き方について綴っています。<br>腰痛・年齢・予定にとらわれず、自分らしく過ごす週末のヒントになればうれしいです。</p>

<p><strong>Beer today, marathon tomorrow?</strong><br>This is a story about finding joy in moderation—and making peace with your limits, one sip at a time.</p>
</blockquote>

<p><span class="bold-red"><strong>🕒 約2〜3分で読めます / 2–3 min read</strong></span></p>

<p><strong>冒頭文/introduction</strong></p>

<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><p>「マラソン前日にビールフェスって、正気か？」<br>自分でもそう思いながら、それでも心が向いたのは、たぶん“無理せずに楽しみたい”という想いがあったから。<br>そんな50代の週末、今回は少しユルく、でも前向きに綴ってみました。</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<p>“Beer before marathon? Am I out of my mind?”<br>Even I questioned myself—but maybe, deep down, I just wanted to enjoy life without pushing too hard.<br>Here’s a laid-back reflection from a weekend that was a little irresponsible… and totally worth it.</p>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/PXL_20250426_043737297-scaled.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-212"/></figure>
</blockquote>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">本文/<strong>body</strong>&nbsp;</h2>

<p>こんにちは、ひろしです。<br>Hello, I'm Hiroshi.</p>

<p><strong>「腰が痛い」</strong><br>"My back hurts."</p>

<p><strong>「明日はマラソン大会」</strong><br>"And I have a marathon tomorrow."</p>

<p>そんな状況の50代が、なぜかクラフトビールフェスに向かう――<br>This 50-year-old guy, despite all that, decides to head to a craft beer festival.</p>

<p>自分でも思いましたよ。<br>Even I thought:</p>

<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><p><strong>「俺、大丈夫か？」</strong><br><em>"Am I seriously okay?"</em></p>
</blockquote>

<p>でも、こう考えたんです。<br>But then I told myself:</p>

<p><strong>「無理しない範囲で楽しめばいいじゃないか」</strong><br><em>"As long as I don't overdo it, I can still enjoy myself."</em></p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/PXL_20250426_050000132-scaled.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-206"/></figure>

<p>会場に着いて、まず衝撃。<br>I arrived at the venue and was immediately shocked.</p>

<p><strong>「座る場所？ もう埋まってますよ。」</strong><br>"Seats? Oh, they're all taken."</p>

<p>腰痛持ちの私に突きつけられた現実は、<br>For someone with back pain like me, reality hit hard:</p>

<p><strong>“立ち飲みでビール4杯セット”</strong><br><em>A four-glass craft beer set... while standing.</em></p>

<p>一口飲むたびに、心の中でこう呟く。<br>With every sip, I muttered to myself:</p>

<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><p><strong>「明日、走れるのか…？」</strong><br><em>"Can I really run tomorrow...?"</em></p>
</blockquote>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/PXL_20250426_044723448-scaled.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-207"/></figure>

<p>でも、不思議と心は満たされていました。<br>Strangely enough, my heart felt full.</p>

<p>若い頃のように勢いで飲むんじゃなく、<br>It wasn’t about chugging beer like in my youth,</p>

<p>**“無理しながらも楽しむ余裕”**がある。<br>It was about <em>"the grace to enjoy, even with limitations."</em></p>

<p>これが、50代の<strong>大人の楽しみ方</strong>なんだと実感。<br>That's what I realized — this is how adults in their 50s enjoy life.</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/PXL_20250426_044456648-scaled.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-209"/></figure>

<p>同世代のみなさん、<br>To everyone around my age,</p>

<p>健康も大事。でも、<strong>心の栄養も忘れちゃいけません</strong>。<br>Health is important, but don't forget to nourish your soul too.</p>

<p>完璧な状況なんて待っていたら、何もできません。<br>If you wait for the perfect conditions, you'll miss out on life.</p>

<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><p><strong>「無理せず、でも楽しむ。」</strong><br><em>"Don't push yourself, but don't forget to enjoy life."</em></p>
</blockquote>

<p>これが、これからの人生を豊かにするコツです。<br>That’s the key to living a fulfilling life from here on out.</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<p>さて、明日のマラソンはどうなるのか…。<br>So, what will happen with tomorrow’s marathon...?</p>

<p>ビール4杯の余韻と、腰の違和感に包まれながら考えています（笑）<br>I'm pondering that while feeling the afterglow of four beers... and my aching back (lol).</p>

<p>来年は、<strong>座れる時間帯を狙って参戦</strong>しよう。<br>Next year, I’ll aim for a time when seats are still available.</p>

<p>そして、マラソン前日はやめておこう…。<br>And maybe... skip the beer fest the day before a marathon.</p>
