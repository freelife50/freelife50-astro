<p>One day, I caught myself thinking, “Retirement’s not that far off.”<br>Looking back, I realized I had lived through the job-hunting ice age, missed the bubble economy, and somehow made it to my 50s.<br>I’ve worked hard all these years, but lately, I’ve started wondering—“Is this really how I want to spend the rest of my life?”</p>

<p>This post is my way of rethinking things—from health and finances to how I want to live.<br>It’s not too late. If you’ve felt the same way, maybe we can pause for a moment together and figure it out.</p>

<h2 id="大変な時代を生き抜いてきた僕らへto-us-who-survived-a-tou" class="wp-block-heading">🌀大変な時代を生き抜いてきた僕らへ<br><small>To Us Who Survived a Tough Era</small></h2>

<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/02d2acd4fa2484edc488054371124173.png" alt="" class="wp-image-389"/></figure>

<p>団塊ジュニア世代。バブルも知らず、就職氷河期をくぐり抜けてきた世代。</p>

<p>「よくそんな大変な時代を生き抜いたね」と言われることがある。<br>People often say, “You really lived through a rough time, huh?”</p>

<p>自分では、それが普通だったから特別だと思ってなかったけど。<br>To me, it didn’t feel unusual — it was just how life was.</p>

<p>でも、気づけば定年もそう遠くない。<br>And yet, here I am — with retirement not so far away.</p>

<h2 id="見直すならまだ体が動く今now-is-the-time--while-our-b" class="wp-block-heading">⏳見直すなら、まだ体が動く“今”<br><small>Now Is the Time — While Our Bodies Still Move</small></h2>

<figure class="wp-block-image size-full"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/dace187eca496fdf9db376f5c25707a0.png" alt="" class="wp-image-390"/></figure>

<p>健康は資産。</p>

<p>体が壊れてからでは遅い。<br>Once your body gives out, it’s too late.</p>

<p>この年齢で壊れたら、もうリカバリーできないかもしれない。<br>If we fall apart now, there might not be time to recover.</p>

<p>だから、今やる。食事、運動、睡眠、そして定期的な検診。<br>That’s why we act now: Eat well, exercise, sleep, and get checkups.</p>

<h2 id="お金との付き合い方を整えるtime-to-rebuild-our-relatio" class="wp-block-heading">💸お金との付き合い方を整える<br><small>Time to Rebuild Our Relationship with Money</small></h2>

<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-full"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/5c5e7d4392e6c6271ac15a3e436daf5f.png" alt="" class="wp-image-391"/></figure>

<p>定年までの時間は思ったより短い。<br>There’s less time left until retirement than we think.</p>

<p>無駄なサブスク、見直してみたらびっくりした。<br>I was shocked when I reviewed my subscriptions — so many unnecessary ones!</p>

<p>家計簿アプリ「マネーフォワードME」で、お金の流れが可視化された。<br>Using the “Money Forward ME” app, I finally saw where my money was going.</p>

<p>外食や趣味も楽しむけど、本当に必要なものにしかお金は使わない。<br>I still enjoy dining out and my hobbies — but I’ve learned to spend only on what truly matters.</p>

<h2 id="逆転プランは行動からreclaiming-our-lives-starts-wi" class="wp-block-heading">🔁逆転プランは“行動”から<br><small>Reclaiming Our Lives Starts with Action</small></h2>

<figure class="wp-block-image aligncenter size-large"><img src="/images/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/591c34c2390e1a44da8a7796827a29f2.png" alt="" class="wp-image-392"/></figure>

<p>逆転したいなら、まずは体とお金を整えること。<br>If you want a comeback, first fix your body and finances.</p>

<p>体が健康ならなんとかなる。<br>If your body’s strong, everything else can follow.</p>

<p>「今さら」なんて言わず、「今から」でいい。<br>Don’t say “too late” — say “from now on.”</p>

<p>今日から始めよう。<br>Let’s start today.</p>

<h2 id="行動の後押し--step-forward" class="wp-block-heading">🚀行動の後押し – Step Forward</h2>

</ul>

<p>僕らはもう一度、人生を“再設計”できる。<br>We still have time to redesign our lives.</p>
